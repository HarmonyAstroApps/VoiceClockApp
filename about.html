About Voice Clock

We built Voice Clock because time management shouldn't require looking at your phone constantly.

Voice Clock Time Speaker is a hands-free time announcer app designed for people with ADHD, accessibility needs, and anyone who wants to stay on schedule without constant screen checking.

The Problem We Solved

Traditional clocks and notifications force you to look at your screen. For people with ADHD, accessibility challenges, or anyone trying to stay focused, this is frustrating. Voice Clock speaks the time to you, so you stay in the zone.

Why Voice Clock Exists

  • For ADHD users: Time-blindness is real. Voice announcements provide external time awareness without phone addiction.
  • For accessibility: Blind and low-vision users get independent time awareness. No need to ask "what time is it?"
  • For multitasking: Cooking, exercising, working — keep your eyes off the screen, ears on the time.
  • For caregivers: Gentle reminders for patients and care schedules.

Our Mission

Make time accessible and stress-free. Whether you need reminders, struggle with time blindness, or just want hands-free awareness, Voice Clock is there.

What Makes Voice Clock Different

  • 15+ languages with natural voices
  • Customizable announcement intervals
  • Minimal battery drain
  • Works offline
  • Built for accessibility
