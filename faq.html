Voice Clock FAQ

Common questions about Voice Clock, answered.

General

How does Voice Clock help with ADHD?
ADHD often involves time-blindness. Voice Clock speaks the time at intervals you set, providing external awareness without screen checking. This helps you stay on schedule and focused.
Does Voice Clock work offline?
Yes, fully. All functionality is local — no internet required for time announcements.
How many languages does Voice Clock support?
15+ languages including English, Spanish, French, German, Japanese, Mandarin, Arabic, Hindi, and more — each with natural voices.

Features

Can I customize announcement intervals?
Yes. Set announcements every 5 min, hourly, or at specific times. Fully customizable.
Is Voice Clock good for accessibility?
Absolutely. Blind and low-vision users can access time independently. Built with accessibility in mind.
